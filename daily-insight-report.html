Executive Summary

Your managers are making decisions
without the full picture.

The financial services landscape moves fast. Regulatory changes, competitor moves, member behavior shifts, and technology disruptions surface daily — across dozens of publications, feeds, and sources that no manager has time to monitor alone.

The Daily Insight Report changes that. Each morning, AI harvests and processes the news that matters — filtered and contextualized for each of your 20 manager roles — and delivers a personalized briefing directly to their inbox. Your branch operations VP sees what's relevant to branches. Your lending team sees what moves rates and risk. Your digital team sees what's reshaping member expectations.

Every insight is also stored in an internal news repository — a growing, searchable library hosted in SharePoint — and ingested into your existing Kasisto knowledge base, so the intelligence compounds over time. The more the system runs, the smarter and more valuable it becomes.

This system is built almost entirely on technology Copper State already licenses — Microsoft Copilot, Power Automate, SharePoint, and Kasisto. The investment is in configuration, intelligence design, and deployment — not new infrastructure.

Technology

Built on what
you already have.

The Daily Insight Report is designed to run on Copper State's existing Microsoft ecosystem. No new enterprise platforms to procure or license — only configuration, integration, and intelligence design.

News Harvesting
Microsoft Copilot + Bing News Already Licensed
Daily automated article collection from curated sources — industry publications, regulatory bodies, competitor news, Arizona business press. Configured via Power Automate with Bing News connector.
AI Processing
Azure OpenAI via Copilot Studio New Config
Each article is processed by GPT-4o — generating a 3–5 sentence summary, extracting 2–3 role-specific insights, and tagging relevance across the 20 role profiles. Prompts are engineered and refined during Phase 3.
Orchestration
Microsoft Power Automate Already Licensed
The central automation layer. Schedules daily runs, routes processed insights to SharePoint, triggers personalized emails to each role, and manages the Kasisto ingestion pipeline.
Email Delivery
Microsoft Outlook Already Licensed
Daily Insight Reports sent as formatted HTML emails via Power Automate. Each of 20 role templates is designed for clarity and scannability — article title, AI summary, and role-specific insights in under 2 minutes of reading.
Repository
SharePoint Online / OneDrive Already Licensed
All articles, summaries, and insights are stored in a structured SharePoint document library — searchable, filterable by topic, date, and role. Accessible to any Copper State staff member with appropriate permissions.
Knowledge Base
Kasisto Already Licensed
Processed insights are ingested into Copper State's existing Kasisto instance on a daily or weekly cadence. Over time, Kasisto becomes a deep institutional knowledge base — every article, every trend, every insight accumulated and queryable by AI.
💡

Estimated ongoing API cost: $300–600/month. Azure OpenAI usage for daily processing of 20–40 articles across 20 role profiles. All other components run on existing Microsoft licensing with no additional per-use cost.
