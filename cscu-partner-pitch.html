Our Intentions

This is not a sales pitch.
It's an offer of partnership.

We believe credit unions are strongest when we support each other. Smaller credit unions often face the same headwinds we've already navigated — with fewer resources to navigate them.

  • We're not here to acquire your members
  • Your member relationships remain yours — always
  • We work in the background; you stay front and center
  • No exclusivity or lock-in

"We've built the infrastructure. The question is — can we put it to work for more Arizona families?"

Our guiding principle

Credit unions exist to serve members, not to compete with each other. If we can help a smaller credit union deliver better products to their members, that's a win for the entire movement.

The Landscape

You're navigating real headwinds.

Scale Pressure

Bigger institutions can offer lower rates and more products. Competing on range requires infrastructure most smaller CUs can't build alone.

Compliance Burden

Regulatory demands keep growing. Keeping policies current, audits clean, and vendors vetted consumes management time and budget.

Talent & Leadership

Recruiting and developing leaders in smaller markets is hard. Without development pipelines, key-person risk grows over time.

Earnings Pressure

Thin margins mean every basis point matters. Balance sheet optimization and fee income diversification are increasingly necessary.

Member Expectations

Members expect mortgage products, investment services, and small business lending. Meeting those needs without a referral network means losing members to banks.

None of these are unique to you. We've faced every one of them.

Lending Solutions

First Mortgage Lending

Your members come to you first. We give you the infrastructure to say yes.

  • Full mortgage origination platform available to your members
  • Your team stays in the relationship — we handle processing and secondary market
  • Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA product set
  • Members see it as a product from their credit union
  • Revenue participation structure to be negotiated
Small Business Lending

SMBs are underserved in many Arizona communities. Your local relationships are the competitive advantage — our credit infrastructure enables the delivery.

  • SBA 7(a) and 504 loan access
  • Lines of credit and term loans for small businesses
  • Credit underwriting and packaging support
  • Business deposit relationships stay with you
  • Shared revenue on funded loans

The principle: Your members should never need to walk into a bank to get a mortgage or a business loan. Let's make sure they don't have to.

Partnership Service 2 of 4

Wealth Management Services

When your members have money to invest, where do they go? Without an in-house offering, the answer is usually a bank or broker — and you lose the relationship.

  • Investment accounts (brokerage, IRA, retirement planning)
  • Life and disability insurance products
  • Licensed advisors serve your members under your brand presence
  • Fee and revenue sharing back to your credit union
  • Program can be co-branded or white-labeled
Why this matters

Wealth management deepens the member relationship in ways that a checking account simply can't. Members who have investments at their credit union rarely leave.

What you gain

  • A full investment program without hiring advisors
  • New non-interest income stream
  • Members keep their whole financial picture at your CU

What your members gain

  • Trusted relationship with someone who knows them
  • Access to investment services they'd otherwise get from a bank
  • Local, Arizona-based support
Partnership Service 3 of 4

Balance Sheet &
Liquidity Support

Deposit Placement

If your balance sheet is deposit-heavy and you need earning assets — or if you have excess liquidity you want to put to work — we can place deposits on our balance sheet and pay a competitive rate.

  • Structured as certificates or term deposits
  • Competitive yield relative to Fed Funds alternatives
  • Helps your asset/liability mix
  • Flexible maturities based on your ALM strategy
Loan Participations

If you need earning assets but lack origination volume in specific categories, we can sell you participations in our loan portfolio — quality asset classes, vetted credit, immediate yield.

  • Consumer, mortgage, or commercial participation options
  • Full underwriting files and documentation shared
  • Choose asset classes that fit your risk appetite
  • Quarterly reporting on participation performance

Bottom line: Your balance sheet should be working harder. Let's find structures that improve your earnings without adding operational complexity.

The Partnership Model

How this actually works.

  • You stay in front. Your brand, your team, your relationship. We never communicate directly with your members without your knowledge and direction.
  • Start where you need it most. Pick one area — lending, wealth, operations — and build from there. No obligation to take everything.
  • Governed by partner agreements. Every service is formalized in writing. Revenue sharing, service levels, and exit terms are all defined up front.
  • No exclusivity. You can work with other partners. We're not asking for lock-in — we're asking for trust, and we'll earn it.
  • Regular check-ins. Quarterly calls between our CEOs to review what's working, what isn't, and what's next.
Our commitment to you

"We will never use this relationship as leverage. If something isn't working, we fix it or we end it gracefully. That's what credit unions do for each other."

We're asking for a chance to be useful — nothing more.
